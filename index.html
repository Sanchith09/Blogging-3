Places Must Visit

Sydney Opera House

I had visited the Opera house in January 26th 2022

It is a mesmerising sight to behold that is one of the main tourist attraction in all of Australia and it ceratinly lives up to its reputation. The vivid and picturesque scenery is wonderful and it is also perfect to take a few picture-perfect shots whilst you are their. The food and diverse cuisine around it are very popular and the diversity of the food is perfect to have as you enjoy the one in a lifetime view. It may not be that engaging for kids as there aren't too many physical activities but it is certainly a crowd pleaser for the parents and an escape from stress and work.

Review On The Movies

Tom and Jerry
Excellent film to culminate MCU ride

I had watched this movie yesterday and this movie was wonderful. Its a childhood classic show finally produced into a child-friendly and heart warmign movie. Watching the original show is recommended just so you know each character's personality and that way the viewing experience will also be much better for you. The whole movie again has Tom as the classic antagonist and jerry tagging along but they used everything from the past in the Tom and Jerry industry to create a spectacular and comedic movie. The movie does go around for 2hrs but regardless it is a fun movie and every minute is worth watching.
